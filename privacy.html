Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: account data (name, email, username, password stored via Supabase Auth), profile data (sport preferences, avatar), and session data (match results, scores, opponents, locations, timestamps). We also collect limited usage data automatically (device type, OS version) solely to improve the service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide and personalize the RallyFlex service, calculate your stats and session history, send transactional emails (e.g. password reset), and improve app performance. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Sharing

Your profile and match results may be visible to other RallyFlex users you play with. We do not share your email address with other users. We work with Supabase (database and authentication) and Expo/EAS (app delivery). These providers access data only as needed to operate the service.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the app's profile screen. Deleted data is purged within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security including encrypted connections (TLS), hashed passwords, and row-level security on our database.

6. Children's Privacy

RallyFlex is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at rallyflex@yahoo.com or use the Delete Account feature in the app.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy questions: rallyflex@yahoo.com
